# MLB-Financial-Analysis
10-Year Study of MLB Revenue vs. Player Payroll (2016-2025).
https://tinyurl.com/MLB-Efficiency-Data 
Project Overview
A 10-year deep dive into the financial mechanics of Major League Baseball. This project tracks the relationship between franchise revenue, cash payroll, and winning percentage across all 30 organizations to identify spending efficiency trends.

Core Analytics
Total Scope: 10 seasons of data (2016–2025).

Mean League Revenue: $313M.

Mean Cash Payroll: $158.45M.

CBT Utilization: Teams averaged 74% of the Luxury Tax threshold, highlighting strategic financial "padding."

Technical Documentation
Tools: Power BI (Visualization), Excel (Data Engineering), DAX (Analytical Measures).

Sourcing: Multi-source aggregation from Spotrac and Forbes.
